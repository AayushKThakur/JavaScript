Breakfast

Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Flour
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 2 large Eggs

Instructions:

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Pour batter onto a hot skillet.
  3. Cook until golden on each side, then serve warm.

Lunch

Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices Bread
  • 2 slices Cheese
  • 1 tbsp Butter

Instructions:

  1. Butter one side of each bread slice.
  2. Place cheese between slices, buttered side out.
  3. Grill in a pan until golden and cheese is melted.
